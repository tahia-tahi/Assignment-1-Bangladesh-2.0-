Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Smart infrastructure, green energy, tech-driven education, fintech growth, and sustainable agriculture.

Sustainability

Renewable energy, eco-friendly industries, green urban planning, sustainable farming, and conservation

Infrastructure

Smart cities, modern transportation, resilient housing, digital connectivity, and efficient logistics.

Empowerment

Digital literacy, women-led enterprises, inclusive policies, skill development, and youth innovation

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

In Bangladesh 2.0, empowerment is at the core. Imagine a country where every individual—man, woman, and child—can dream without limits. Women, who are already the backbone of our nation’s economy, will lead this revolution. We will ensure they have access to education, technology, and financing. Microfinance was just the beginning. It is now time to create micro-entrepreneurs in every corner of Bangladesh.We will embrace digital literacy, enabling every Bangladeshi to navigate the digital economy. From the villages of Rangpur to the bustling streets of Dhaka, innovation will empower even the most marginalized among us.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
